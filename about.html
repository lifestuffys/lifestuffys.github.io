Origins

Started on February 16, 2023, LIFE Stuffys is a freelance art business. Any illustration, whether hand-drawn or digital, or logo you need, we sketch your ideas to life!

Ian Flores Escalante

Hecho en México, Raised a Texan

Initially sketching for fun, I found an aesthetic joy in the skill of art. That fond hobby led me to become an illustrator and digital artist.

For me, nothing is better than a perfectly made image.

My only “formal” education that I have received was in my middle school years (haha I know, not very spectacular). So for the most part, I’m self-taught.

Mr. Bubbleman

Not a Real Person

Back in Ian’s sophomore year of high school (2018-2019), I became a lil doodle drawn around the margins of notes during classes. And since I looked bubble-like, my name was created: Mr. Bubbleman. Sadly I was thrown away at the end of the year, so my initial adventures are gone… yet I have thrived!! I pop up whenever and wherever I can hehe

My more prominent role came when LIFE Stuffys was in development (July 2022). While drafting the idea of a freelance graphic design business, Ian gave me the role to become the bootiful face of LIFE Stuffys. And so, I took it and made it my goal to make yall laugh!
